HotDogs

"Frank's Flame Grill is a vibrant and energetic food truck that specializes in serving up delicious, flame-grilled hotdogs bursting with flavor. With a menu featuring a variety of toppings and condiments to choose from, customers can customize their hotdog to their liking. The truck is known for its sizzling hotdogs, friendly service, and inviting atmosphere that keeps customers coming back for more."

What we are know for

"Frank's Flame Grill is best known for its signature 'Flaming Frank,' a mouthwatering hotdog that is flame-grilled to perfection and topped with a special blend of spicy jalapenos, tangy onions, and zesty chipotle mayo, offering a fiery kick that sets it apart from other hotdogs on the menu.
